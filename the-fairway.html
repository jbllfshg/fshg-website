The Fairway

Local produce. Above par.

Perched above the greens with sweeping 180° views, The Fairway serves seasonal cuisine in a relaxed club setting — wood-fired pizza, char-grilled steaks, fresh seafood, and a kitchen that leans on the best local produce.

Book a table View the menu

A relaxed table with a view over the greens

Set inside Penrith Golf Club, The Fairway is where Western Sydney comes for an easy lunch, a long dinner, or a celebration that runs late. The menu travels from pub favourites — schnitzels, burgers and parmis — to Thai-spiced barramundi, seared octopus and dry-aged steaks finished with café de Paris butter.

Whether you've just come off the course or you're here for the food alone, there's a seat with your name on it seven days a week.

Lunch & dinner, 7 days — open from 11:30am daily.
Members & visitors welcome — members enjoy a discounted price on every dish.
Private functions — celebrations, wakes and corporate events catered in-house.
