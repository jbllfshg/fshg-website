The Jack & Cleaver

Schnitzels, burgers & a cold one.

Tucked inside Pennant Hills Bowling Club, The Jack & Cleaver is the neighbourhood's go-to for a great schnitzel, a proper burger, and a cold drink overlooking the green. Warm, unpretentious, and always delicious.

Book a table View the menu

Your local, done properly

The Jack & Cleaver is the kind of place you end up at on a Tuesday and again on Saturday. Hand-crumbed schnitzels you can load up however you like, char-grilled steaks, wood-fired pizzas, and a weekend-only menu that brings out the Greek lamb shoulder and the pad thai.

Pull up a seat by the green, grab a cold one, and settle in — lunch and dinner, every day of the week.

Lunch & dinner, 7 days — open from 11:30am daily.
Members & visitors welcome — members enjoy a discounted price on every dish.
For the weekend — an extra menu of specials, Friday nights and all weekend.
