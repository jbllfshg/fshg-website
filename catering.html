Good to know

Delivery & pickup

🚚

Free delivery over $350

Orders of $350 or more deliver free within 8km of your chosen venue. Under $350, a flat $50 delivery fee applies.

🛍

Free pickup, 11:30am–8pm

Collect your platters — hot or cold — free of charge between 11:30am and 8:00pm from The Fairway or The Jack & Cleaver.

🕗

After-hours pickup

Hot or cold pickup outside of 11:30am–8:00pm can be arranged and may incur an additional charge.

📅

48 hours notice

Please place your order at least 48 hours ahead so our kitchen can prepare everything fresh for you.
