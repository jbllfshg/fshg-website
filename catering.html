Catering, the easy way

From our kitchen in Seven Hills to your home, office or venue. Choose a ready-to-serve package for a team lunch or a celebration, or build your own order from our à la carte platter menu — all delivered, or ready to collect.

1

Pick a package or platters. Browse our corporate and private-event packages, or add individual platters and set the quantities.

2

Choose delivery or pickup. Tell us your venue, date and time. À la carte orders over $350 deliver free within 8km; packages include delivery.

3

Order or enquire. Order platters online in a few taps, or send a package enquiry and we'll tailor it to your event.

Good to know

Delivery & pickup

🚚

Free delivery over $350

Orders of $350 or more deliver free within 8km of your chosen venue. Under $350, a flat $50 delivery fee applies.

🛍

Free pickup, 11:30am–8pm

Collect your platters — hot or cold — free of charge between 11:30am and 8:00pm from The Fairway or The Jack & Cleaver.

🕗

After-hours pickup

Hot or cold pickup outside of 11:30am–8:00pm can be arranged and may incur an additional charge.

📅

48 hours notice

Please place your order at least 48 hours ahead so our kitchen can prepare everything fresh for you.
